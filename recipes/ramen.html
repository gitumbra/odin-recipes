Ramen Recipe

A delicious bowl of ramen!

Made with miso, ramen noodles, mushrooms, and green onions.

The recipe is simple and fast to make!

Ingredients:

  • 3 Â½ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • A small package of mushrooms, your choice
  • A handful of sliced green onions to top each serving bowl
  1. Boil water in medium sauce pan
  2. While water boils, sautee mushrooms in another pan
  3. When water boils, throw in ramen noodles and miso broth stock. Taste to liking
  4. Boil noodles for 7 minutes, stir often, reduce heat if boiling over
  5. Slice green onions and add cooked mushrooms to broth noodle mix
  6. Serve and enjoy!
