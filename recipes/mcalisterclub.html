McAlister's Club Recipe

Delicious complex club made with freshly sliced ham and butterball turkey.

The recipe is simple and fast to make!

Ingredients:

  • Sliced wheat
  • Mayo and honey mustard
  • Lettuce and tomato
  • Cooked bacon slices
  • 20z of sliced ham
  • 2oz of sliced turkey
  • 1 slice of cheddar
  • 1 slice of swiss
  1. Toast 3 pieces sliced wheat (bottom, middle, top)
  2. Apply mayo to bottom piece, then apply honey mustard to middle and top piece
  3. Lay ham, turkey, cheddar, then swiss to bottom toast
  4. Add tomato, sliced bacon, and lettuce to middle piece
  5. Stack top piece ontop of middle, and then middle/top onto bottom piece for a 3-tiered sandwich
  6. Cut corner to corner, apply toothpicks to each piece to avoid disassembly
  7. Plate and enjoy!
